PROJECT SUPERVISOR-STUDENT MATCHING : 2020-21

  • 35 of the 45 project pairs have been matched to a supervisor they nominated.
  • Individual students who did not nominate a student partner have been given a student partner, mostly within programmes.
  • A version of the Gale-Shapley algorithm for producing stable marriages was implemented by Götz Pfeiffer as part of the Schoolmaster system (thanks Götz!) and used by him to produce a matching between supervisors and student pairs.
  • Students pairs are welcome to arrange to permute supervisors, subject to two contraints: (1) the permutation must not alter the workload of any supervisor; (2) and all students and supervisors involved in the permutation must agree to it.
